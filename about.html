Our Story

About Us

Born in the streets. Built on conviction. Draped in gold.

The Beginning

We didn't find streetwear.
We became it.

Shastra Wear was born in 2024 in the lanes of Malegaon — where concrete meets culture and every corner has a story. Founded by a collective of designers and street artists who were tired of choosing between luxury and authenticity, we set out to bridge that gap permanently.

The name "Shastra" (शस्त्र) comes from Sanskrit, meaning weapon or instrument of power. It reflects our core belief — that what you wear is not just clothing. It is your armour. Your declaration. Your identity forged in thread and stitch.

From our first drop of 50 hand-numbered pieces to a community of thousands across India, every step has been intentional. We are not a trend. We are a movement — slow-built, values-led, and made to last.

What Drives Us
Our Mission

To create premium streetwear that empowers individuals to wear their truth without compromise. We exist to prove that luxury and street culture are not opposites — they are the same language spoken with different accents. Every piece we create is a testament to that belief: bold, uncompromising, built to endure.

Where We're Headed
Our Vision

To become the defining voice of South Asian streetwear on the global stage. We envision a world where the streets of Nashik, Mumbai, Delhi, and Chennai are spoken of in the same breath as New York, Tokyo, and London — and Shastra Wear stands at the heart of that conversation. Culture made to conquer.
